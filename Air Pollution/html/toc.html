Table of Contents [ TOC ]

The TOC or table of contents, is to the left of the map.

  • Each entry is called a 'legend' and represents a map 'layer' or 'theme'
  • If you click on a legend, that layer is called the active layer, or selected layer
  • Its display (rendering) properties can be controlled using the Legend Editor, and the
    legends can be reordered using Layer Control
